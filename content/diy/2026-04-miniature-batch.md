# Miniature batch — Anycubic Photon Mono 4K

A batch of tabletop miniatures printed on the Photon Mono 4K, tuned for fine
detail on faces and weapons.

## Settings

50 micron layers, standard grey resin, supports on tips and thin weapon
parts only.

## Result

Clean detail on faces, minor layer lines on large flat armor plates — next
batch I'll try a slower exposure on the first few layers.
