# ReelMate

An AI-driven platform that streamlines creating and distributing short-form
video content across major social media platforms. Transforms text content
into videos, automates cross-platform scheduling, provides engagement
analytics, and includes a Telegram bot for content-approval workflows.

## Stack

FastAPI, AsyncIO, OpenAI, Telegram Bot API
