# First sparring round

Notes from my first real sparring round after a few months of bag work and
pad sessions — what went well, what I want to drill next.

## What went well

Kept my guard up and moved off the back foot instead of squaring up.

## What to work on

Combos under pressure fall apart — need more drilling on the 1-2-slip.
