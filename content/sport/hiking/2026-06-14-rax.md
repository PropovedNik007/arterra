# Rax — Heukuppe via Ottohaus

A day hike up Rax from Vienna. Started at the Preinerwiese trailhead, up to
Ottohaus, then the ridge walk to Heukuppe (2007 m) and back down the same
way.

## Route

Preinerwiese → Ottohaus → Heukuppe → Ottohaus → Preinerwiese. Well-marked,
some exposed sections near the summit ridge.

## Conditions

Clear, warm, light wind on the ridge. Started early to avoid the afternoon
crowds on the plateau.

## Gear notes

Trail shoes were fine for this route; no scrambling gear needed for this
line.
